FAQ

Q: How can I become a member of the swimming club?
A: Visit us at the swimming club booth on campus or visit our website to fill up the membership form and to find out more information/application procedure.
Q: What degree of ability do I need to join the swimming club?
A: Our club welcomes swimmers of all skill levels, inlcuding beginners, intermediate and advanced.
Q: Do members have access to coaching?
A: Yes, APU Swimming Club provides certified coaching for skill development and advice.
Q: What are the advantages of joining a swimming club?
A: Pool access, training sessions, coaching competitions, social activities and skill development are all available.
Q: What swimming activities or program does the club provide?
A: Training sessions, leisure swimming, skill improvement, competitions and social activities are all part of the program.
Q: Is there an age limit for joining the swimming club?
A: No, our club is open to members of all ages, however particular activities may have distinct division or age group.
Q: How frequently does the the swimming club get together for practices or events?
A: The frequency of meetings varies, but we attempt to schedule frequent practices, events and gatherings. Generally, on a weekly basis.
Q: As a member, what equipment or gear do I need to bring?
A: Members are welcome to bring their own swimsuits, googles, swim caps and any other equipments needed for practice or competition. APU campus store sells official APU swimming team iteam which can be purchased there.
Q: Does the club provide competitive swimming opportunities?
A: Yes, APU Swimming Club provides competitive swimmimng options such as meet or tournament competition. All members get access to information reagrding this in a timely manner.
Q: How much does it cost to join the swimming club?
A: Membership is completely free. While most activities are free, certain events may charge a modest fee. Enjoy multiple advanatges with no upfront cost.
